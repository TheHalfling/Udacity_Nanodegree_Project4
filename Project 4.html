Reflection

Oregon has long been a Blue State when it comes to the Presidential elections, so finding that the Democratic Candidates had a greater support base was not unexpected. I was also unsurprised to see that the top counties and zip codes are those that make up and surround the Portland area as it is the most population dense. It was interesting to see that even in these urban areas there was still a strong showing for Republican candidates, which I found to be unexpected. Overall the information presented matched expected results and was an interesting peak into the financial side of the election.
